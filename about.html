My Favorite Food

This is dendeng batokok , it is Sumatera Barat's specialty food. Maybe you know about rendang (Padangnese cuisine) that everyone loves, dendeng have it's own unique flavour. It has a spicy kick that makes everybody who eat it become addicted. Give it a try if you go to Indonesia. Find a Padangnese Restaurant and ask for it, trust me you won't regret it !!
